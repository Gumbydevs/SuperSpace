Instructions

  • Destroy asteroids and enemy ships to earn points and credits.
  • Visit the shop (B) to upgrade your ship with new weapons and ships.
  • Hold Left Shift for afterburner speed boost (uses energy).
  • Switch weapons with Q/E or use number keys 0-9 for direct selection.

Controls (Desktop)

  • Move: Arrow Keys / WASD
  • Shoot: Spacebar
  • Afterburner: Left Shift (hold)
  • Weapon Switch: Q (previous) / E (next)
  • Direct Weapon Select: 0-9 number keys
  • Shop: B key
  • Player Profile: P key
  • Mute Music: M key
  • Mute SFX: N key
  • Show/Hide Menu: Esc key
